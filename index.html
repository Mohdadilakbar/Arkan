In March 2014, Arkan Overseas Co. Established his own contracting establishment. In the year 2014, Nassar Al-Hajri joined with Nassar Falah Al-Hajri with the spirit of moving the business to the next level; it was converted into a joint liability company in the name of Arkan Contracting Company (Arkan).

Arkan is now one of the leading installers of RTR pipes in addition to building construction, electro-mechanical activities and building trades mainly servicing Saudi Aramco.

Arkan has its huge potential to be a high growth segment of General Contracting, civil, mechanical construction and Maintenance Company.
